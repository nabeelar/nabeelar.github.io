﻿I recently graduated from New York University with a PhD in Computer Science, where I was advised by Dr. Rumi Chunara.
My doctoral research focused on developing statistical and machine learning approach for modelling the spread of diseases at high spatial resolution.

Before starting my PhD, I worked as a researcher at Information Technology University and Lahore University of Management Sciences with Dr. Umar Saif and Dr. Lakshminarayanan Subramanian.
During this time, I also helped Punjab Information Technology Board, in their initiative to improve disease surveillance in Punjab, Pakistan and developed their disease forecasting and outbreak detection systems. ﻿
